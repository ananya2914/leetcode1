Track Your LeetCode Journey

A Chrome extension that helps you track your LeetCode progress, analyze your performance, and stay motivated.

Get Started

Features

Progress Tracking

Track your solved problems, difficulty distribution, and topic-wise progress.

Streak Tracking

Maintain your daily streak and stay motivated to solve problems regularly.

Real-time Updates

Get instant updates on your progress as you solve problems on LeetCode.

Installation

1

Download the Extension

Download the latest release from the Chrome Web Store or build it from source.

2

Install in Chrome

Open Chrome and go to chrome://extensions

3

Enable Developer Mode

Toggle on "Developer mode" in the top right corner of the extensions page.

4

Load the Extension

Click "Load unpacked" and select the extension directory.
